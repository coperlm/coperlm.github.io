数论小芝士

Posted on 2024-06-03 Edited on 2024-06-13

欧拉定理：https://oi-wiki.org/math/number-theory/fermat/

\(phi = n-p-q+1\)，故 \(c=m^{phi+2} mod \ n\) ，由欧拉定理可得 $c^{phi}  mod n $

则 $cm^{2} mod  n $

直接开根即可

威尔逊定理：\((p-1)!\equiv -1\ mod\ p\)

第一步爆破n：

得到 \(n\)，直接分解得到 \(p\) 和 \(q\)

第二步：

已知 \(M\equiv m*(p-q-1)\ mod\ n\)

因为 \(p\mid n\)，所以 \(M\equiv m*(p-q-1)\ mod\ p\)（放缩）

得到方程 \[ \begin{cases} (p-1)! \equiv -1\ mod\ p\\ M\equiv m*(p-q-1)\ mod\ p \end{cases} \] \(\therefore m\equiv \dfrac{M*(p-1)!}{(-1)*(p-q-1)!}\)

Initially, lets just look at a question.

In this question, we know high bite of p and low bite of q, and asked to find all the bite of p and q

In the first step, we can access low bite of p by low bite of q, because p*q=n

we get \(p_{low}=n*q_{low}\ (mod\ 2^{256})\)

This question has convert to how to get p(1024bit) by high bite of p(1024-724=300) and low bite of p(256bit), totally 556 bites
